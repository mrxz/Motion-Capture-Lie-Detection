.bmocap (binary motion capture format)

int32	FrameRate
int32	SegmentCount
int32	FrameCount

After that, for each frame:

boolean	FrameType (0 = normal, 1 = npose)
int32 	TimeCode
int32	JointCount

After that, for each joint:

single	Position x
single	Position y
single	Position z
single  Orientation x
single	Orientation y
single	Orientation z
single	Orientation w
